# Project 02 – Digital Footprint Assessment of Deel

## Objective

Conduct an Open-Source Intelligence (OSINT) investigation to map the publicly exposed digital footprint of Deel. The objective was to identify the organization's public infrastructure, corporate information, technology stack, external security posture, and potential attack surface using only passive intelligence collection techniques.

---

## Investigation Scope

This investigation included:

- Company overview
- Corporate structure
- Domain registration analysis
- Digital footprint mapping
- Technology stack identification
- Security posture assessment
- Threat assessment
- Intelligence assessment

---

## Methodology

The investigation followed a structured OSINT methodology, beginning with corporate identification and domain validation before progressing to digital footprint mapping, infrastructure analysis, technology identification, and external security posture assessment. Only publicly available information and passive reconnaissance techniques were used throughout the investigation.

---

## Tools Used

- VirusTotal
- URLScan.io
- BuiltWith
- OpenCorporates
- ICANN Lookup
- SecurityHeaders
- MXToolbox
- Shodan

---

## Skills Demonstrated

- Open-Source Intelligence (OSINT)
- Digital Footprint Analysis
- Infrastructure Mapping
- Technology Profiling
- Corporate Intelligence
- Threat Intelligence
- Security Posture Assessment
- Intelligence Reporting

---

## Key Findings

- Identified Deel's publicly exposed digital infrastructure.
- Mapped the organization's technology stack and externally accessible services.
- Verified corporate registration and domain ownership information.
- Assessed email security configuration and web security headers.
- Identified no evidence of malicious activity or critical public exposure during the investigation.
