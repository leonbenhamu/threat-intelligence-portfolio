# Project 01 – Phishing Infrastructure Investigation

## Investigation Status

In Progress

## Objective

Investigate a suspected phishing infrastructure and determine whether it is likely being used for credential theft or malicious activity.

## Intelligence Questions

1. Is the domain malicious?
2. What organization is being impersonated?
3. When was the domain registered?
4. What infrastructure supports the operation?
5. What indicators of compromise can be identified?
6. What level of risk does the domain represent?

## Methodology

This investigation will use:

* VirusTotal
* URLScan
* WHOIS
* AbuseIPDB

## Deliverables

* Threat Intelligence Report
* Infrastructure Analysis
* IOC List
* Risk Assessment
